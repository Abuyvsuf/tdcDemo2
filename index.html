About The Debate Circle

Editorial clarity, civic purpose, youth leadership.

TDC began as a small after-school forum where students debated civic issues in a safe, structured space. As participation grew, we expanded into county-level circuits, mentorship labs, and national showcases connecting schools across Kenya. Today our platform hosts debates, monologues, roundtable conversations, student leadership debates, and livestreamed conversations — bridging the gap between young people and the issues that shape their world.
