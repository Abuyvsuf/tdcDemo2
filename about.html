About Us

A platform for informed, accountable young citizens

The Debate Circle turns conversations into civic confidence for high school and university students across Kenya.

Origin Story

Built around young voices and national purpose.

The Debate Circle (TDC) is a conversation platform started with the aim of brewing positive engagement across different demographics of young people to build a knowledgeable, action-ready, and accountable citizenry. The platform has galvanized partnerships in media and corporate sectors to forge an organization that creates meaningful agendas for young people — furthering them through conversations and debates. Under the UN Global Goals and ESGs, TDC shapes the new generation (14–18 years) to understand and view the world through the 17 SDGs — creating the perfect avenue to brew debates, conversations, and a nationwide agenda for the youth.
