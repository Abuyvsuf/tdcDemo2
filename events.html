Regional Events — 2025 Season


Region 1

Nairobi / Kiambu Region

February 2025 · Nairobi

Schools from Nairobi and Kiambu counties competed in the opening regional qualifier of the 2025 season. Debaters tackled SDG-themed motions on economic inclusion, climate resilience, and gender equality. Over 20 schools turned out for one of TDC's most competitive regional rounds, with teams engaging KDIC-sponsored financial literacy debates.

📷 Nairobi / Kiambu Region
Region 2

Mount Kenya Region

April 2025 · Nyeri

The Mount Kenya regional was a landmark moment in the 2025 season — highlights went viral on TikTok with over 141,900 likes. Debaters from schools across Nyeri, Kirinyaga, and Meru counties debated with remarkable energy and depth. The tournament ran two full days of competitive rounds with adjudication clinics held alongside.

📷 Mount Kenya Region
Region 3

Rift Valley / Nakuru Region

April 2025 · Nakuru

Schools from the Rift Valley descended on Nakuru for a high-stakes regional qualifier. Debate motions explored youth unemployment, food security, and digital infrastructure — pressing SDG themes relevant to the region. The tournament concluded with a joint adjudication feedback session for all participating schools.

📷 Rift Valley / Nakuru Region
Region 4

North Rift / Kitale Region

May 2025 · Kitale

Students from the North Rift engaged in spirited debates at this regional qualifier. St Monica's Girls High School was among the notable participants. The region tackled themes around climate change, food sovereignty, and women in leadership — issues that resonate deeply in this agricultural belt of Kenya.

📷 North Rift / Kitale Region
Region 5

Coastal Region

May 2025 · Mombasa / Diani

The Coastal regional, held in partnership with NCBA Bank, brought together schools from Mombasa, Kilifi, Kwale, and Taita-Taveta counties. Aveon School in Diani was among the competing institutions. Debates focused on ocean economy, youth empowerment, and SDG 14 (Life Below Water) — topics uniquely relevant to Kenya's coastline.

📷 Coastal Region

Lower Eastern Region

May 2025 · Machakos

Schools from Machakos, Makueni, and Kitui counties gathered for the Lower Eastern qualifier. Participants debated food systems, water access, and youth civic participation in a region known for its strong student debating tradition.

📷 Lower Eastern Region

Nationals

TDC National Debate Finals 2025

August 2–3, 2025 · Nairobi

The 2025 National Debate Finals marked the culmination of months of competition across 12 regional circuits. The event brought together regional champions, educators, financial sector leaders, and public officials. KDIC's Director of Bank Resolution David Irungu addressed students on financial literacy as a national development strategy. Jamhuri High School emerged as the 2025 National Champions in a season that saw students debate savings culture, insurance awareness, economic inclusion, and the role of youth in Kenya's financial future. The finals weekend included adjudication clinics, alumni panels, and TDC's post-tournament African Themed Gala Night.

Teachers' Professional Development Workshops

TDC invests in the educators behind the debaters. Our teachers' workshops equip coaches with argument structure, adjudication standards, and SDG-aligned debate curricula.

Workshop

Teachers Workshop at the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE)

2022 · Nairobi

TDC partnered with the Nairobi Securities Exchange to host a teachers workshop focused on equipping educators with debate facilitation skills rooted in financial literacy. Teachers from schools across Nairobi participated in sessions designed to help them run effective debate clubs and introduce students to conversations around Kenya's capital markets and economic citizenship.

📷 NSE Teachers Workshop 2022
Workshop

Kiambu Region Teachers Workshop at St Paul's University

2022–2023 · Kiambu

Held at St Paul's University in the Nairobi–Kiambu corridor, this workshop brought teachers from Kiambu county schools to build their capacity as debate coaches. Sessions covered argument structure, adjudication standards, and how to embed SDG-aligned debate motions into a school co-curricular programme.

📷 Kiambu Teachers Workshop
Workshop

Teachers Workshop in Partnership with NCBA Bank, Nairobi

2023 · Nairobi

In collaboration with NCBA Bank, TDC delivered dedicated teacher training merging financial literacy with debate facilitation. Educators were equipped to guide students through motions on saving, credit, digital banking, and financial inclusion — all framed through the SDGs.

📷 NCBA Nairobi Teachers Workshop
Workshop

Coastal Region Teachers Workshop, NCBA Bank Partnership

2023 · Mombasa

TDC took its teacher training programme to the Coast in partnership with NCBA Bank. Educators from Mombasa, Kilifi, and Kwale counties participated in sessions covering debate facilitation, adjudication, and how to develop region-specific SDG debate motions relevant to coastal livelihoods, ocean economy, and tourism.

📷 Coastal NCBA Teachers Workshop
